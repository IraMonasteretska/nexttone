Hands-on with new planning optimization in Dynamics 365 for Supply Chain

In this video we will review activation and execution of master planning using new planning optimization feature. This feature allows companies to run master planning calculation in Azure cloud, outside of company’s environment to avoid decrease in system performance. We will also run the same master plan with planning optimization turned off. We will then compare the results produced by these two processes side-by-side. Lastly we will take a look at plan filters.
